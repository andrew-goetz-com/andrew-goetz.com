About Andrew Goetz

AI consultant with over two decades of hands-on experience in technology, system administration, and software development.

I'm Andrew Goetz, founder of AgiGUI and an AI consultant with over two decades of experience in technology. I specialize in training businesses and professionals to unlock the power of AI, particularly large language models like ChatGPT and Copilot, to radically improve productivity and streamline operations.

Throughout my career, I've led enterprise IT initiatives, deployed secure infrastructure, and pioneered innovative automation techniques. My journey spans startups, corporate IT, and SaaS environments, but my true passion lies in education and empowering others to thrive in the age of AI.

I currently work as an IT Administrator for a global pharmaceutical company. At the same time, through AgiGUI, I offer tailored AI workshops that teach prompt engineering, smart tool integration, and data workflows—all designed to give your team immediate, measurable results.
